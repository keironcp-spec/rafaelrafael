Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies and local storage technologies are small pieces of data stored in your browser when visiting websites. They are used to ensure website functionality, remember your preferences, and improve the user experience.

2. Technologies We Use

OnlyCRM uses the following storage technologies:

LocalStorage:

  • Interface theme (dark/light/blue)
  • Selected interface language (en/ru/uk)
  • Authorization tokens (access/refresh)
  • Cookie consent preference

Cookies:

  • Service cookies for WebSocket connections (Socket.IO)
  • Session cookies for security

3. Cookie Categories

Strictly Necessary: ensure the operation of core Service functions (authorization, security, WebSocket connections). Cannot be disabled.

Functional: save your preferences (theme, language) for convenience.

All cookies we use are necessary for the Service to function or for user convenience. We do not use advertising or marketing cookies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect Service functionality. A consent banner will be shown on your first visit to the website.

5. Policy Changes

We may update this Policy. The current version is always available on this page.
